Remote Health Monitoring

Connect with a doctor virtually


You can directly talk to one of our doctors either by video conferencing or by chatting. You also have the opportunity to talk to one of your local health cares or doctors if they are listed in our website. You can also seek medical information from pharmacists.

Renew your Prescription Online.

You can now renew your prescription completely online. You will just need to enter your medial information with your patient id. In addition to renewing your prescription, you can also view your previous medications.


Furthermore, you can also order any medicines from your nearest hospital or pharmacy.

Add a Hospital


By adding a hospital to this site, patients can interact directly with the hospital. For example patients can view their medical reports, pay bills online and even interact with the staff of the hospital. This way, patients can see doctors and seek medical informtaion online.
